# Pinouts and wire colours

## Accessory bus

### Colours

General:

- White: RS485 A/+
- Green: RS485 B/-
- Red: 5V
- Black: Ground

Extraction airflow sensor:

- White with orange stripes: RS485 A/+
- White with blue stripes: RS485 B/-
- Orange with white stripes: 5v
- Blue with white stripes: Ground

### 5 pin DIN pinout

From left to right, looking at socket with locking/alighnment pin pointing up.

1) 5V
2) RS485 B/-
3) n/c
4) RS485 A/+
5) Ground
